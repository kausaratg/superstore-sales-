# 📊 Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project analyzes historical retail sales data from the Superstore dataset to uncover business insights and build an interactive Power BI dashboard for decision-making.

The dashboard tracks key performance indicators (KPIs), identifies sales trends, highlights top-performing products and regions, and provides actionable recommendations to improve business performance.

---

## Project Objectives

The goal of this project was to answer key business questions, including:

* Which region generates the highest sales?
* Which product category is the most profitable?
* Who are the top customers?
* Which customer segment contributes the most revenue?
* How have sales changed over time?
* Which shipping method is used most frequently?

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains historical retail transactions, including:

---

## Key Insights

* Technology generated the highest sales and profit.
* The West region recorded the highest revenue, followed by the East region.
* Consumer customers contributed the largest share of total sales.
* Standard Class was the most frequently used shipping method.
* Sales showed seasonal fluctuations throughout the year.
* Furniture generated lower profit compared to Technology and Office Supplies, indicating opportunities for improvement.

---

## Recommendations

* Invest more in the Technology category due to its strong profitability.
* Launch targeted marketing campaigns in lower-performing regions.
* Review pricing strategies and operational costs for the Furniture category.
* Improve delivery efficiency while maintaining Standard Class shipping.
* Monitor monthly sales trends to prepare for seasonal demand.
